LOCALE

When you're trying to find a D&D group, your local game store is another great place to start. Most gaming stores and hobby shops have at least one of two things: a bulletin board where groups can post flyers looking for new players or groups of people playing D&D Adventurers League. Both can land you a seat in a game.

MUG & MAGIC

For those in the vicinity of Angeles, Pampanga, visit Mug & Magic, your local coffee shop and tabletop game store!
